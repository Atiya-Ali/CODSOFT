In this project I've created a music genre classification system along with my peers using the knowledge of machine learning. Weather App

The weather app project aims to provide users with accurate and up-to-date weather information based on their location or a specified location. The app will feature a clean and intuitive user interface, allowing users to easily access current weather conditions, forecasts for the upcoming days, and additional details such as humidity, wind speed, and precipitation probability. Utilizing APIs for weather data retrieval, the app will ensure reliable data sourcing and seamless integration of weather updates. With responsive design principles in mind, the app will be accessible across various devices, ensuring users can check the weather conveniently whether they are at home or on the go.
